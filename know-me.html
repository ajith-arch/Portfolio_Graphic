I am a Senior Graphic Designer with 6+ years of experience crafting visual systems that connect brand strategy to real-world outcomes. My work spans university communications, marketing campaigns, editorial design, and digital storytelling, with a strong focus on clarity, consistency, and visual impact.

I approach design as both craft and communication. Whether developing a brand identity, shaping a campaign rollout, or building high-volume marketing assets, I prioritize typography, hierarchy, and composition so each piece feels intentional and easy to understand.

Across healthcare, e-commerce, education, and fintech, I have designed for both print and digital channels, adapting creative direction to different audiences without losing brand cohesion. From editorial layouts to campaign graphics and web assets, I focus on work that is both aesthetically strong and production-ready.

I collaborate closely with marketers, content teams, developers, and stakeholders to move projects from concept to final delivery with speed and precision. My workflow is grounded in practical systems thinking, version control, and clean asset handoff so creative quality scales across teams.

My core toolkit includes Adobe Creative Suite: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, and After Effects. I use these tools to build cohesive visual narratives across branding, editorial, motion, and campaign design - creating work that not only looks polished, but performs with purpose.
